We here at Vets R Us pride ourselves on our services when working with your animals. Although we specialise in dog treatments, we are more than capable of treating other suburban pets including but not limited to cats, birds, guinea pigs etc. We do not currently have any rural clients but will happily take a look at your farm animals if that is necessary. If you are unsure if we are able to help you, do not hesitate to head to the contact page on this website and fill out the form, or use the phone number or email address listed there to get in touch with us.

Our Services

General Check Up

We will take in your pet for a short time ensure all their basic needs are being met and that there is nothing to worry about. Cost varies based on how much work needs doing.

Emergency Room

We handle emergencies that occur any time of day as we have at least one vet on site at all times.

Surgery

If necessary we can perform surgery on your pet. We keep a pristine, sterile environment for all of our surgeries to ensure nothing can go wrong that could have been prevented.
